Bio

Mitch Zachry is a folk songwriter and guitarist born in New Zealand and currently based in Melbourne, Australia.

After a run of experimental singles, Mitch Zachry began to define his existential folk sound on his debut album, Love Love Love Swim Swim Swim Be Be Be, released in May 2024. With his new album War In The Garden, that sound returns more focused, thoughtful, and singular.

War In The Garden is a raw and honest collection of songs exploring deeper parts of the human experience — the desire for freedom, the sadness of time passing, and fear in the face of total uncertainty. Zachry combines effortless fingerpicked guitar with clever turns of phrase and unique song structures to create an album that is minimalist, alive, and intricate.

Album out now.
