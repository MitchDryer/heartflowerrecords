Heartflower Records is an independent record label based in Wellington, New Zealand.

Here creativity is understood as the natural flow of unrestricted life, and the products of creativity are simply symbols within the heart of this creative force.

Although flowers are admired as beautiful expressions of nature, our concern and attention rests within the abundant totality of the entire garden.

Money, streaming, and play counts are not problems and do not need to be demonised, but they can be compassionately recognised as manifestations of fear and are therefore not worth serving.
